These raised proceeds are described in the evidence as allocated to autonomous driving, battery systems, intelligent cockpit platforms, and to build a second plant.  \n- 2025 disclosures / status: the evidence shows Xiaomi increased 2025 delivery targets (350,000 vehicles in some sources) and continued factory expansion (Phase II completion mid‑June 2025; Phase III land purchased in 2025) [14], [15].\n\nWhat the USD 10 billion commitment refers to in the evidence\n- Announcement language reported at the time (March 30, 2021) and repeated in subsequent reporting frames the figure as an investment over ten years.
