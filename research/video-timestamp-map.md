It is the best source for documentary/analytical sequences.

5. **Best cinematic B-roll** comes from `su7-reveal.mp4` (scenic driving, product beauty) and the SU7 finale section of `leijun-speech.mp4` (4:24-5:00, dark cinematic silhouettes and coastal driving).
