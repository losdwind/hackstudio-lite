## Tesla Model 3 Sales Impact After SU7 Launch

### Key Findings
- Tesla sold ~657,000 vehicles total in China in 2024 (+8.8% YoY overall, but Model 3 specifically was hit)
- **Oct-Dec 2024 comparison**:
  - Tesla Model 3: 52,241 units
  - Xiaomi SU7: 69,697 units (Oct: 20,726 / Nov: 23,156 / Dec: 25,815)
- **December 2024**: SU7 outsold Model 3 for the first time definitively
- **April 2024 - January 2025 cumulative**: Xiaomi SU7 delivered 162,384 units vs. Tesla Model 3's 152,748 units
- **January 2025 (early data)**: SU7 outsold Model 3 by roughly 3:1 (15,600 vs. 5,200 units)

### Competitive Positioning
- SU7 base price ~9% cheaper than Model 3 (215,900 vs. 245,900 RMB)
- SU7 CLTC range advantage: 700 km vs. 606 km
- SU7 targets the same demographic: tech-savvy, style-conscious urban buyers
