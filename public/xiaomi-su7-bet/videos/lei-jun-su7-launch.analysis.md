## Xiaomi SU7 Launch Event Summary

On March 28, 2024 in Beijing, Lei Jun unveiled the Xiaomi SU7, Xiaomi’s first electric vehicle, highlighting its performance, intelligence, and safety features. The presentation detailed significant investments – 19.1 billion in 2023 R&D with a planned 24 billion in 2024, and a 136.3 billion cash reserve – demonstrating Xiaomi’s commitment to the automotive industry.  The SU7 boasts impressive specs including a 2.78-second 0-100km/h acceleration (Max version), 800V charging, and advanced driver-assistance systems powered by NVIDIA DRIVE Orin.  Multiple configurations were announced, with pricing starting at 215,900 yuan, and pre-orders opened immediately following the event. Lei Jun emphasized Xiaomi’s focus on a seamless user experience, integrating the car with the Xiaomi ecosystem and offering a range of customization options.
