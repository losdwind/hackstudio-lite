## B-Roll Usage Rules
1. **Zero clip repetition** — each clip used only once across all 5 parts
2. **No text frames as backgrounds** — avoid frames with on-screen text/titles
3. **Clean frames only** — no watermarks, no blurry frames, no audience obstructions
4. **Official sources only** — all 9 videos from official Xiaomi/CNBC/BBC channels
5. **Match emotional register** — somber b-roll for heavy moments, dynamic b-roll for victories
